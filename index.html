Written Directions (for Mac)

  1. Have a computer with Python (comes preinstalled on Macs) and a Gmail Account. Also, if you have 2 Factor Authentication on for your Gmail turn it off (or use a Gmail that does not have 2FA turned on)
  2. Go to myaccount.google.com and click security on the menu to the left
  3. Scroll down to "Less secure app access" and turn this ON. (can turn it back OFF after)
  4. Click the "Download Bot" on the download section of this website
  5. Drag the file onto your desktop
  6. Unzip the defundEmailBot folder
  7. On your desktop, open the unzipped folder and open the info.py file in a text editor (TextEdit on a mac)
  8. Fill in your info and save the file
  9. Open "Terminal" on Mac
  10. Type in "cd Desktop" and hit Enter
  11. Type in "cd defundEmailBot" and hit Enter
  12. Type in "python ./defundEmailBot.py" and hit Enter
  13. Check your sent emails.

**Make sure you enable less secure apps on your google account first! (Steps 1-3 below)

Written Directions (for PC)

  1. Have a a Gmail Account and if it has 2 Factor Authentication ON then turn it OFF just while you run the bot (or use a gmail that does not have 2FA turned on)
  2. Go to myaccount.google.com and click security on the menu to the left
  3. Scroll down to "Less secure app access" and turn this ON. (can turn it back OFF after)
  4. Now you have to download Python. Go to Python.org/downloads and click the big yellow "Download Python" button
  5. The installer will pop up on the bottom of your screen, click it twice.
  6. There will be a pop up that says "Install Python", MAKE SURE TO check the "Add Python 3.8. to PATH box
  7. Then click "Install Now" and wait for it to download. When it is done it should say "Setup was successful"
  8. On this website, go to the Download section and click "Download Bot"
  9. Drag the file onto your desktop
  10. Right click on the file you dragged and hover over "7-Zip" then click "Extract files..."
  11. Uncheck the box that has "defundEmailBot\" inside then press "Ok". Now you should have a folder on your Desktop called "defundEmailBot"
  12. Open the folder and right click on the "info" file.
  13. Scroll down to "Open with" and if "Notepad" is there click it. If "Notepad" is not there than click "Choose another app" then click "more apps". Now scroll until you find "Notepad" and click it.
  14. Fill in your info and save the file (you can do this with Ctrl + s)
  15. Now you can close the info file. Then, hit the windows button on your keybboard and type "command prompt" and click the application
  16. Type in "cd Desktop" and hit Enter
  17. Type in "cd defundEmailBot" and hit Enter
  18. Type in "python ./defundEmailBot.py" and hit Enter
  19. Check your sent emails.
